A Movie for Today

Rating: 60 %
Awards: 1 win & 4 nominations.
Length: 214 mins

The film centers mostly around the personal and professional life of Thomas "Stonewall" Jackson, a brilliant if eccentric Confederate general, from the outbreak of the American Civil War until its halfway point when Jackson is killed accidentally by his own soldiers in May 1863 during his greatest victory.

Events

  • July 21st , 1861 Thomas Jackson earns his nickname of "Stonewall" at the First Battle of Bull Run

  • May 2nd , 1863 Stonewall Jackson's arm is amputated

  • May 10th , 1863 Stonewall Jackson dies

Directed by Ron Maxwell

Starring Donzaleigh Abernathy, Mark Aldrich, George Allen, Keith Allison
