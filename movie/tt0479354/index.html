Chronicle of an Escape (2006)

In 1977, the goalkeeper of the Almagro team Claudio Tamburrini is illegally kidnapped from his home, arrested by the government forces and sent blindfolded to Sere Mansion, a clandestine detention center. His acquaintance Tano, who had been tortured for a long period, had falsely accused Claudio of being a revolutionary. Along the months, Claudio is kept nude and handcuffed together with the prisoners El Gallego, El Vasco and Guillermo Fernndez and submitted to all sort of humiliations and tortures, until the night they decide to escape from their imprisonment in the old mansion.

Events

  • November 23rd , 1977 Claudio Tamburrini is abducted by the Argentinian secret police

  • March 24th , 1978 Claudio Tamburrin and his cellmates escape during a thunderstorm
